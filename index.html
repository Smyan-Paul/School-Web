Campus Life



school ground

Was established in 1978

Our school ground is mainly used as a cricket ground but we also play football and hockey there.



school library

Was built in 1980

Our library consists of 500 books in 3 languages English, Hindi and Sanskrit with also some award winning books as mouse in the rooftop.



school auditorium

Was built in 1982

Our auditorium has a capacity of about 1500 people. All our programs and award functions happen here. There are also 15 cameras to record functions
